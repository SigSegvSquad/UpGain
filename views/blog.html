How it works...

Its pretty simple.

You earn money by uploading your resources that you think people might find useful. When people find your uploaded document and spend time on your page, it generates revenue... because we show ads on each page.

The revenue which is generated on the whole website is collected from google at the end of the month and distributed to the contributors. To do it fairly, we keep track of things like how useful people find your document or how much time they spend on your page. This means, the more relevant your contributions are, the more you earn!

We also show your estimated earnings on your profile, we update it every few days. The ad revenue is never constant. Sometimes you'll earn $3-4 per 1000 impressions on your contributions(total impressions include impressions from all contributions, not just one) and sometimes you'll earn less.

What can I upload?

You may upload your assignments, projects, presentations, documents, notes or anything that you think may get you views. You can share your contributions with your friends in school/college.

At the end of the day, what really matters is how helpful your contributions are to students :)
